What is 31 Days of Dean?

Dean, the great was born in July of 1994. It is said that when River Phoneix died, River's creative spirit was reincarnated into the King that we all know and love. It's been 28 years and Dean still does not know who River Phoenix is or what they did.

Dean is like nothing the world has ever seen. Legend has it, that he died at birth but upon crawling up to the flaming gates of hell, he looked death straight and in the eyes and said "Give me my crown or run me my 10."

The Devil was so afraid he sent the newborn back up to Earth in hopes that he could reach retirement before he had to face King Dean again.

G.O.A.T.

Dean is known in most circles as the 'G.O.A.T'™. Which circles, you ask? Don't ask. We're not currently accepting questions at this time.

What makes Dean Great?

  1. Can correctly pronounce 'Taika Waititi' the first time
  2. Can swim. Not fast, but he gives a lot of effort.
  3. Has cool shoes.
  4. Can, at any given point, name at least 4 Zodiac signs.
  5. Knows the name of all four Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.
  6. Can parallel park without outside assitance.
  7. Has dislocated and relocated his own shoulder more than once in his life.
  8. Can solve a Rubik's cube in under 2 minutes or your money back.
  9. Is not afraid of snakes or spiders.
  10. Is appropriately afraid of possums and racoons.

Need I say more?

Dean uses his skills for good. On the weekends he helps elderly girl scouts cross the road and fists fights drunk FedEx workers under the Mississippi bridge where no one can hear their screams.

Many say that the King is flawed, that his ambitions are too grand and his will too inhuman. But I believe, Dean can save the world.
